Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At QuickGas, our primary goal is your satisfaction and safety. Since we operate on a Pay on Delivery model, we ensure you are happy with the product before any transaction is completed.

1. Cancellations

You may cancel your order at any time before the delivery rider reaches your location at no cost. If the rider has already arrived, we appreciate a valid reason for cancellation.

2. Damaged or Faulty Cylinders

If you receive a cylinder that is leaking, dented, or has a faulty valve:

  • Inform the delivery personnel immediately for an instant exchange.
  • If the fault is discovered after delivery (within 24 hours), contact our support at +254 700 000 000 for a free replacement.

3. Incorrect Weight

We guarantee the weight of our cylinders (6kg, 13kg, 22kg, etc.). If you suspect the weight is incorrect at the time of delivery, you have the right to ask the rider to weigh the cylinder using their portable scale before payment.

4. Refund Processing

Since payments are mostly cash or M-Pesa on delivery, refunds for "change of mind" are not processed once the seal is broken. Refunds are only issued in the event of documented technical failure of the equipment provided by QuickGas.
