Immediate Action Steps

1
NO FLAMES: Do not smoke, use matches, or create any open flames.
2
NO SWITCHES: Do not turn on/off lights or use electrical appliances (even phones near the leak).
3
VENTILATE: Open all doors and windows to let gas disperse.
4
ISOLATE: Turn off the gas regulator if it is safe to do so.
5
EVACUATE: Move everyone outdoors and call for help from a safe distance.
