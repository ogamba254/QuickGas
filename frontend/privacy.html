Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Quick Summary

We only collect the data necessary to deliver your gas safely. We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we use industry-standard encryption to protect your location and payment details.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our delivery services, we collect the following:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Location Data: Your GPS coordinates and physical address for delivery accuracy.
  • Order History: Details of previous gas sizes and brands purchased.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly to:

  • Process and deliver your gas orders.
  • Send SMS updates regarding your delivery status.
  • Improve our service based on your order patterns.

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your location data is encrypted and only accessible to the assigned delivery partner during the active delivery window.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact our data protection officer at privacy@quickgas.com.
